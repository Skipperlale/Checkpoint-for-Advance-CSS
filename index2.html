This Privacy Policy (Policy) describes how Clear Wage (Clear Wage, we, us, or our) collects, uses, and discloses your information (Information) in connection with your use of our payroll processing and related services (collectively, the Services).

Information We Collect

Client Information: This includes your name, business name, contact information, and tax identification number.

Employee Information:This includes your employees names, addresses, Social Security numbers, wage and tax withholding information, and benefit enrollment data.

Usage Data:This includes information about how you and your employees interact with the Services, such as login information, IP addresses, and pages accessed

How We Use Your Information

Provide and improve the Services

Process payroll for your employees

Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Communicate with you about the Services

Send you marketing or promotional materials (with your consent)

Analyze trends and improve the security of the Services

Sharing Your Information

We may share your Information with third-party service providers who help us operate the Services. These service providers are contractually obligated to keep your Information confidential and secure. We may also disclose your Information if required by law or to protect the rights, property, or safety of ourselves or others.

Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to protect your Information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We retain your Information for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with legal and regulatory requirements, or resolve disputes.

Your Choices

Access You may request access to your Information by contacting us.

CorrectionYou may request that we correct any inaccurate Information about you.

Deletion You may request that we delete your Information.
